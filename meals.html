# Standard Breakfast


The Standard Breakfast Cuisine supplied includes the usual staple diet of India. It is majorly of the dishes from the South of India. The proper and Clean food like Idli, Vada, Upma and pongal are served, with an essence of a homely masala. Served along with Chutney, Sambar and Ketchup. The Price rates for the quantity given are reasonable. In non-veg brekafast, they got eggs, bread, butter and omelette. Idli is one of the most healthiest and popular South Indian breakfast dish. These are soft, light, fluffy idlis served with Sambar and Coconut Chutney is not only tasty but very healthy as well. Ben Pongal is a South Indian breakfast made with rice and yellow moong lentils. This porridge like savory dish is laced with cumin, asafoetida and black pepper. Not to forget the lovely aroma of ghee, which is used for tempering these spices.

The South Indian Thali Has the homemade essences of the spices which has Main food-rice, Curries, papad, Pickles, Curd, Sambar, Rasam. In The North Indian Thali there would be mainly Roti, With Potato masala, dal along with curries.
